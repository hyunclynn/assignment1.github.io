THE AREA OF SINGAPORE

Sentosa Island, also known as Sentosa and formerly known as Pulau Blakang Mati, is an island located at the Central Region, Singapore. It was once used as a British military base and a Japanese prisoner-of-war camp. In 1972, the island was renamed Sentosa and was planned to be a popular tourist destination. Consisting of the globally known Universal Studios, S.E.A Aquarium, Adventure Cove Waterpark & its scenic beaches, Sentosa gives you the full range of an outdoor vacation you cannot miss.

NIGHT ACTIVITIES

1) Catch a PARANOMIC view during the cooling night weather on the Skyline @ Sentosa with your loved ones & top it off with a SWOOSHING Luge ride suitable for all ages!

2) Or how about catching Sentosa's MAGICAL show. The first of its kind, Wings of Time is the only permanent night show in the world set in the open sea. Set against a signature backdrop and a larger-than-life water screen, audiences can look forward to a contemporary storyline weaved together with an epic experiential fusion of the latest multimedia effects, including 3D projection mapping, state-of-the-art lasers, robotic water fountains and giant water jets, pyrotechnics.
